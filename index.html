Planning Your Travel

Both Phoenix (PHX) and Flagstaff (FLG) have commercial airports

www.skyharbor.com

www.ifly.com/airports/

If you are flying privately or by helicopter, try Cottonwood or Sedona Airports. We recommend renting a car at the airport. Uber/Lyft is virtually nonexistent once you get to Sedona and there are very few taxis. Having your own vehicle will give you the freedom to explore Sedona. We recommend booking in advance.

If you prefer, there are shuttles from PHX to Sedona. Groome Transportation is one we recommend.

groometransportation.com/sedona/

Sedona is a 2 hour drive from PHX and a 1 hour drive from FLG.

* At VIP Luxury Sedona, we offer off-street parking and can accommodate up to 6 vehicles.

Weather And Packing

Sedona weather fluctuates. It is typically warmer during the day and cooler at night. Packing layers is essential, even in spring and summer. We suggest:

  • Hiking shoes
  • Flip Flops
  • Running Shoes
  • A swimsuit & coverup
  • Hat
  • Shorts and t-shirts
  • Sweatshirt or Light Jacket
  • Sweater/Wrap
  • Sandals
  • Tall rubber boots (for walking around by the creek)

Sedona is very casual, although you may want to dress nicer for a few restaurants in the evening. We also recommend making restaurant reservations in advance of your stay

During Your Stay

* Please turn on “ Wifi calling ” to make and receive calls while on property.

To turn on the spa, go down the stairs out the back door and turn right. The buttons for the spa are located on the back corner of the house. About 30 minutes before you are ready to soak, please only touch the SPA button once and and a red light will illuminate to indicate that the spa is heating up.

Please try not to touch the jets in the hot tub. They are sensitive and go off balance, and stop working. Thank you so much.

When you are finished using the spa, please press the SPA button only once and the red light will turn off.

We keep the pool clean with a mechanical pool vacuum connected to a hose in the pool. If you do remove it while swimming, we ask that you return it to the pool when you are finished.

Note: Vortex Pools comes once every week to clean the pool.

If you are curious about the cloth devices held down by a rock on either side of the pool, we have found this to be incredibly useful in saving small animals and insects that fall into the pool.

PLEASE ALWAYS WEAR SHOES, FLIP FLOPS OR SLIPPERS (especially at night) around the pool and in the house. Although we have monthly pest maintenance, the Arizona desert is notorious for scorpions. Please be careful!
